FAQs

As a student activity agency and community, it's our responsibility to address some common queries to better serve you.

Who can participate in SBS activities?

SBS activities are open to all students who are enrolled at the participating educational institution

What are the benefits of participating in SBS activities?

Participating in SBS activities can enhance your social skills, provide new learning opportunities, and contribute to your personal growth.

How can I stay updated on upcoming SBS activities?

Subscribe to the SBS newsletter, follow us on social media, and check the website regularly for updates.

Who can I contact if I have questions or concerns about an SBS activity?

Are there any prerequisites for participating in certain activities?

Some activities may have specific requirements or prerequisites, such as skill levels or previous experience. These will be clearly stated in the activity description.

Will I receive recognition for participating in SBS activities?

Yes, you can earn certificates and recognition for your participation in SBS activities. These can be valuable additions to your resume or college applications.

How do I register for an SBS activity?

You can register for an activity by clicking on the Get involved button on the activity's page and following the prompts to complete your registration.

Are there any opportunities to showcase my creative projects through SBS?

Yes, we have events and platforms dedicated to showcasing student work. Keep an eye out for these opportunities or propose your own event.

What if I need to cancel my participation in an activity?

Cancellations should be made as soon as possible by contacting the activity organizer directly.
